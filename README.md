# FoodTruth

**Packaged Food Label Intelligence Platform**

FoodTruth is an early-stage product-engineering project focused on helping people understand packaged food labels with more clarity.

The project is currently under active development.

---

## Current Status

FoodTruth Engine v0.1 is being developed and tested privately inside the codebase.

More details will be shared after the core product experience is complete.

---

## Principle

FoodTruth does not tell people what to eat.

It helps people understand what they are buying.
