# FoodTruth Product Vision

FoodTruth is a packaged food label intelligence platform that helps people understand what food labels are really saying.

It analyzes nutrition facts, ingredient lists, serving sizes, additives, and marketing claims to generate a clear, educational label report.

FoodTruth does not tell people what to eat. It helps people understand what they are buying.

## Product Positioning

FoodTruth is not a diet app, medical app, or food judgement tool.

It is a consumer label-intelligence platform designed to make packaged food labels easier to understand.

## Core Promise

The front label sells. The back label reveals.

FoodTruth helps users decode the back label with clarity, transparency, and context.
