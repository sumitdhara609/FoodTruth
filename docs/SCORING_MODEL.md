# FoodTruth Scoring Model

FoodTruth Score is a transparent 0–100 score based on label clarity, nutrition load, ingredient complexity, serving-size transparency, and marketing claim alignment.

## Score Ranges

- 85–100: Excellent Label Clarity
- 70–84: Good, with Minor Concerns
- 50–69: Mixed Profile
- 30–49: High Concern
- 0–29: Very High Concern

## Scoring Factors

- Sugar load
- Sodium load
- Saturated fat load
- Calorie density
- Fiber support
- Protein support
- Ingredient complexity
- Additive indicators
- Marketing claim risk
- Serving size reality

## Philosophy

The scoring system is educational and transparent.

It is not a medical safety rating.

A low score does not mean a product is medically unsafe. It means the label may show concerns such as high sugar density, high sodium load, weak ingredient clarity, or claim mismatch.
