# FoodTruth Safety Principles

FoodTruth is an educational label-intelligence tool.

It does not provide medical advice, diet plans, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

FoodTruth avoids fear-based language and does not label foods as medically safe or unsafe.

The platform focuses on label literacy, nutrition transparency, ingredient clarity, and marketing claim awareness.

## Language Principles

FoodTruth should say:

- This label shows high sugar density.
- This product has a high sodium load.
- This claim may need closer verification.
- This serving size may make the nutrition values appear smaller than realistic consumption.

FoodTruth should not say:

- This food is harmful.
- Do not eat this.
- This product causes disease.
- This food is medically unsafe.

## Disclaimer

FoodTruth is for educational label literacy only. It is not a substitute for professional medical, nutritional, or dietary advice.
